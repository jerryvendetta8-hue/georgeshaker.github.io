About Dr George Shaker

George is an NHS Urology Registrar with over 8 years of experience, holding the FEBU, MRCS, and BAUS membership. Frustrated by the administrative burden facing clinicians daily, he began building AI-powered workflow tools that solve real problems in real clinics.

His tools are now used across NHS trusts and private practice, saving clinicians hours of admin every week. He has 3 first-author publications and 13 international presentations, and is passionate about the intersection of clinical medicine and technology.

His mission: give clinicians time back for what matters most — patient care.
