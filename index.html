Letter Review Assistant

Drafty

The Problem

Every admin session, you sit down with 10–15 typed letters waiting for review. Each one takes 5–8 minutes. Errors slip through. Safety netting gets missed. You leave at 7 PM, exhausted.

The Superpower

Spots spelling errors, flags missing PSA results or follow-up plans, checks medication doses, verifies safety netting, highlights vague language. Confirms whether the letter is ready to send.

“Reviews your letters in 5 seconds. Catches what you miss.”

🎙

Voice-to-Letter Converter

Voxie

The Problem

The dictation cycle is broken. You dictate after each patient. The secretary types it days later. You review and amend. They re-type. Days pass. Letters pile up. Patients wait.

The Superpower

You speak naturally about a consultation. Voxie converts 60 seconds of natural speech into a polished, NHS-format clinic letter — ready for review in seconds. Bypasses the secretary bottleneck entirely.

“Talk for 60 seconds. Get a complete letter.”

🔍

Results Management Assistant

Tracker

The Problem

You request a CT KUB. Two weeks later, you can't remember if the result came back. You request a PSA. Three weeks later, you discover it was 12 and the patient hasn't been contacted.

The Superpower

Maintains a live tracker of all pending investigations. Alerts you when results are overdue. Flags abnormal values. Drafts patient and GP letters automatically. Creates an audit trail for governance.

“Never lose a result again. Sleep better at weekends.”

🩸

Haematuria Clinic Workflow

Haemy

The Problem

Haematuria clinics are admin-heavy. You see 12–15 patients in a session. Each needs flexible cystoscopy, USS, urine cytology, and a follow-up plan. Tracking what each patient needs is a nightmare.

The Superpower

Complete haematuria clinic management — tap-based chip interface for fast data entry, automatic risk stratification (low/intermediate/high), generates investigation plans based on guidelines, creates clinic letters via API in seconds.

“Haematuria clinics, simplified. From referral to letter, in one tool.”

🧠

Pre-Clinic Briefing Tool

Briefy

The Problem

You walk into clinic having never seen the referrals. You spend the first 2 minutes of each 15-minute slot reading. That's 24 minutes per clinic of reading time you could spend with the patient.

The Superpower

The night before clinic, paste in your patient list and referrals. Briefy reads everything and gives you a one-page summary per patient — key issue, relevant history, recent investigations, and what to focus on.

“Walk into clinic ready. Read 12 referrals in 10 minutes.”
